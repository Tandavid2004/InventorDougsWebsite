FIT AT 75: Unlocking the Secrets to Lifelong Health and Mobility

Why do some people at 75 stay vibrant, active, and drug-free while others struggle with mobility and chronic issues? Is it all in the genes, or do lifestyle choices make the real difference? Doug Malewicki, a self-proclaimed "geezer," has spent decades unlocking the secrets to aging well, proving that staying active is the key to remaining capable. From running his age in miles every birthday—most recently 76 miles at 76—to kicking off each New Year with a mountain run matching the year's number, Doug embodies his philosophy. In FIT AT 75, he shares real-life anti-aging strategies, inspiring stories from others who have defied expectations, and practical, entertaining insights to help anyone make 75 the new 50. Packed with humor, visual proof, and simple, actionable tips, this book challenges the notion of "normal" aging. If you're ready to take control of how you age, FIT AT 75 is your blueprint for a longer, healthier, and more active life!

CLONIES are captivating, mind-bending snapshots where the same person appears twice in a single photo—without any Photoshop manipulation. Chapter 6 introduces beginners to fundamental techniques, while Chapters 7-9 reveal advanced tricks for creating truly eye-catching and artistic compositions—including SELFIE CLONIES. Best of all, capturing these images is an exciting, interactive experience that sparks joy and amazement for everyone involved. Whether you're young or old, CLONIES offer a unique way to have fun with photography and unleash your creativity!
